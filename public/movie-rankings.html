The Best Movies According to Drake

My top 3 movies of all-time

Baby Driver

Fantastic music and cast. The action is fast-paced and the humor is witty.

Moulin Rouge

I'm not usually a fan of musicals, but this one is a must see. The way it perfectly blends a melody of contempary songs (for the time), and Ewan McGregor's performace really make it a masterpiece.

Star Wars: A New Hope

This is the essential starting point for modern sci-fi cinema.
